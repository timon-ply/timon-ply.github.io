Support

Contact and issue reporting

Use this page for app issues, account questions, data requests, accessibility feedback, and questions about the documents hosted on this site.

Email support

What to include

  • App name: NeonRoutine or Kalvenda
  • Platform: Android, iOS, web, or desktop
  • App version, if available
  • A short description of the issue
  • Steps that reproduce the problem
  • Relevant screenshots or error messages

Account and data requests

Include the app name, the account email or sign-in provider, and the request type. Do not send passwords or authentication codes.
