Support

Report issues directly and include the details needed to investigate.

Use this page for app issues, account questions, legal-document questions, data deletion requests, accessibility feedback, and store-review support.

Email support

What to include

  • App name: NeonRoutine or Kalvenda
  • Platform: Android, iOS, web, or desktop
  • App version if visible in the app or store listing
  • Short description of the issue
  • Steps that reproduce the problem
  • Any relevant screenshots or error messages

Account and data requests

For account deletion, privacy, or data questions, include the app name, the account email or sign-in provider, and the request type. Do not send passwords or authentication codes.

Accessibility feedback

Accessibility reports are useful when they include the screen name, the assistive technology used, expected behavior, and what blocked the interaction.
