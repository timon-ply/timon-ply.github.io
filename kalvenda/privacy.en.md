# Privacy Policy for Kalvenda

Last Updated: March 2026

## 1. Introduction
This Privacy Policy applies to the use of the mobile application **Kalvenda** (the "App"), developed and operated by Timon Polley ("we", "us", or "our"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information in accordance with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and other applicable laws (including the TDDDG and DDG).

## 2. Information We Collect & Lawful Basis (Art. 6 GDPR)
We collect the minimum amount of data necessary to operate the App effectively. Our processing is based on the following legal grounds:

- **Account & Profile Data**: When you create an account, we collect your email address, display name, and authentication data (via Google or Email/Password).
  *Lawful Basis: Performance of a Contract (Art. 6(1)(b) GDPR).*

- **Social & Group Data**: Your created groups, events, alerts, reactions, and membership information are stored securely on our servers (Google Firebase/Firestore) to synchronize across your devices and with other members of your groups.
  *Lawful Basis: Performance of a Contract (Art. 6(1)(b) GDPR).*

- **Location & Map Data**: When you add a location to an event, coordinate data (latitude/longitude) is stored in our database and displayed on an interactive map powered by OpenStreetMap. No continuous or background location tracking is performed; location data is only stored when you explicitly add a location to an event.
  *Lawful Basis: Performance of a Contract (Art. 6(1)(b) GDPR).*

- **In-App Purchases & Subscriptions**: Managed via RevenueCat, which processes purchase receipts and subscription status on our behalf. Payments are processed securely by the respective app stores (Apple App Store / Google Play). We do not have access to your payment details.
  *Lawful Basis: Performance of a Contract (Art. 6(1)(b) GDPR).*

- **Advertisements**: We use Google AdMob to serve ads. AdMob may collect device identifiers (e.g., Advertising ID), interaction data, and create interest-based profiles for targeted advertising purposes. Before AdMob is activated, your explicit consent is requested.
  *Lawful Basis: Explicit Consent (Art. 6(1)(a) GDPR, § 25(1) TDDDG).*

- **Usage Data & Analytics**: Firebase collects anonymized crash reports and usage metadata (e.g., app version, device model, error logs) to improve the App's performance and fix bugs. This data is only collected after your explicit consent.
  *Lawful Basis: Explicit Consent (Art. 6(1)(a) GDPR).*

- **Push Notifications**: Used to alert you about group invitations, new events, and updates. Firebase Cloud Messaging (FCM) processes a device token for notification delivery. Notifications require your explicit permission (granted via your OS settings).
  *Lawful Basis: Explicit Consent (Art. 6(1)(a) GDPR).*

**Mandatory provision of data**: Providing your email address and authentication data is necessary to create an account and use the App. Without this data, the service cannot be provided. All other data (event location, ad consent, analytics consent, push notifications) is voluntary.

## 3. Third-Party Services & Sub-Processors (Art. 28 GDPR)
The App uses third-party services that may process data on our behalf. We have accepted Data Processing Agreements (DPAs) with these sub-processors:

- **Google LLC (Firebase Auth, Firestore, Cloud Functions, Firebase App Check, Firebase Cloud Messaging, Google AdMob, Google Sign-In)**: Database hosting, authentication, app integrity verification, push notifications, and ad serving. Google's DPA incorporates EU Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs) for transfers to the US. Data is hosted in the EU (europe-west3) where technically possible.
- **RevenueCat, Inc.**: Subscription and in-app purchase management. RevenueCat processes purchase receipts, subscription status, and entitlements. RevenueCat's privacy policy: https://www.revenuecat.com/privacy
- **Apple Inc. / Google LLC**: Processing of App Store / Play Store transactions.
- **OpenStreetMap tile providers**: When the map view is displayed, tile requests are sent to OpenStreetMap servers. Tile providers may log request metadata including IP addresses. See the [OpenStreetMap Privacy Policy](https://wiki.osmfoundation.org/wiki/Privacy_Policy) for details.

## 4. International Data Transfers (Art. 46 GDPR)
Google LLC is established in the United States. Transfers of your personal data to the US are safeguarded by Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs) as adopted by the European Commission, incorporated into Google's Cloud Data Processing Addendum. Details are available at: https://cloud.google.com/terms/data-processing-addendum

## 5. Automated Decision-Making & Profiling (Art. 22 GDPR)
We do not make automated decisions with legally significant effects on users. Google AdMob may use automated profiling based on device identifiers and interaction patterns to select personalized advertisements. This profiling is subject to your prior consent (see Section 2) and can be withdrawn at any time via the App's privacy settings.

## 6. Your Rights (Art. 15–22 GDPR)
Under the GDPR, you have extensive rights regarding your data:

- **Right of Access & Data Portability (Art. 15 & 20)**: You can request an export of your personal data at any time by contacting us.
- **Right to Erasure (Art. 17)**: You can permanently delete your account and all associated personal data directly within the App's settings.
- **Right to Rectification (Art. 16)**: You can edit or modify your profile and content within the App.
- **Right to Restriction of Processing (Art. 18)**: You may request that we restrict processing of your data in certain circumstances.
- **Right to Object & Withdraw Consent (Art. 21 & 7)**: You can withdraw your ad or analytics consent at any time via the App's privacy settings. Push notifications can be disabled via your device's OS settings. Withdrawal of consent does not affect the lawfulness of processing based on consent before withdrawal.
- **Right to lodge a complaint (Art. 77)**: You have the right to lodge a complaint with the competent supervisory data protection authority. The authority responsible for our activities is:
  **Landesbeauftragte für Datenschutz und Informationsfreiheit Nordrhein-Westfalen (LDI NRW)**
  Kavalleriestraße 2–4, 40213 Düsseldorf, Germany
  www.ldi.nrw.de

## 7. Data Retention
We retain personal data only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this Privacy Policy.

- **Account & Social Data**: Retained until you delete your account. Upon account deletion, your personal profile data is removed. Content you shared in groups may be anonymized or deleted depending on the context, to preserve group integrity for other members.
- **Event Location Data**: Retained as part of the event record until the group or event is deleted by its creator.
- **Analytics & Crash Reports**: Retained by Google Firebase for up to 90 days.
